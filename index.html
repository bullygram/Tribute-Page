Timeline Of A. P. J. Abdul Kalam:

  • 15 Oct 1931 - A great scientist is born to a boat-owner's family
  • 1960 - Became a scientist with DRDO
  • 1969 - Joined space research at ISRO
  • 18 Sep 1980 - Launched India's first indegenous satellite
  • 1981 - Padma Bhushan
  • 1970s - The Devil and the Valiant
  • 1980s - Builds crucial missiles for India
  • 1990 - Padma Vibhushan
  • 1992 to 1999 - Nuclear Testing
  • 1997 - Bharat Ratna
  • 25 July 2002 - Becomes the 11th President of India
  • 24 July 2007 - End of Presidency
  • 27 July 2015 - The People's President passes away
